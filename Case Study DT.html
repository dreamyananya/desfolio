DoodleTales

View on Behance

Role➔ UI/UX Designer

Duration➔ 3 weeks

Tools➔ Figma, Ibis Paint X, Adobe Capture

Quick Impact➔ A whimsical storytelling platform transforming simple doodles into interactive narratives that engage users creatively.

Problem➔

Casual doodlers lack a playful, intuitive way to bring their scribbles to life as digital stories that are easy for kids to comprehend.

Objective➔

Create a delightful, easy-to-use interface that transforms user sketches into shareable, narrative-driven experiences.

  • Research method➔
  • Informal interviews with doodle enthusiasts
  • Discussions with parents and young learners
  • Insight highlights➔
  • Users enjoy features that spark imagination and creativity
  • Interactive storytelling increases engagement
  • Accessible story formats improve usability and inclusivity
  • Metrics➔
  • 82% users preferred creativity-driven prompts
  • 71% parents wanted stories in multiple formats
  • Users spent 40% more time on interactive storytelling experiences
  • 3 out of 4 participants favored visual + narrative experiences over basic drawing tools

  • Impact➔
  • Encouraged creative self-expression in a playful interface
  • Boosted user engagement by making storytelling accessible to kids in video, audio as well as text format
  • Reflection➔
  • While curating for kids as primary users, clarity and playful styling should be balanced to keep their attention
  • Designing for younger audiences requires simplifying interactions without making the experience feel limiting or repetitive
