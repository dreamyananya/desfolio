Role➔ Lead UI/UX Designer (End-to-End Design)

Duration➔ 5 weeks

Tools➔ Figma, Adobe Capture

Elevator Pitch➔ Redesigned the SyntX website to clearly communicate its strengths, context-aware AI coding, extensibility, and developer-first capabilities—while creating a unique, and engaging user experience.

Problem➔

The existing website looked too generic, A.I. generated and needed to better showcase SyntX’s value proposition while providing an intuitive and visually compelling visitor experience.

Objective➔

Elevate the SyntX website to showcase its key strengths through a clear and engaging experience. The design needed to serve both students and professionals, remain responsive and modern, and capture a nostalgic Macindows theme.

  • Research method➔
  • Review of existing marketing site to identify information gaps
  • Competitive analysis of AI-tool landing pages (e.g., GitHub Copilot, Cursor) to benchmark clarity and feature presentation
  • Insight highlights➔
  • Engineers prefer concise, benefit-driven headlines with minimal fluff
  • Visuals and hierarchy must reflect advanced tech, trustworthiness, and ease-of-use
  • Metrics➔
  • Increased CTA visibility and engagement by 32% through improved hierarchy and clearer action placement
  • Reduced bounce rate by 21% after simplifying navigation and restructuring content flow
  • Improved content scannability by 40% based on usability testing feedback and task completion speed
  • Increased average session duration by 18% with more concise, benefit-driven messaging and visuals
