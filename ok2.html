InkStrip | Bridging Comic Creators & Readers


View on Behance

ROLE: UX Designer | UX Researcherr

TOOLS: Figma, IbisX Paint

DURATION: 3 Weeks

ELEVATOR SUMMARY: A dedicated platform empowering comic creators with the right tools, while offering fans an interactive, personalized reading experience.


Problem & Objective

PROBLEM: Comic creators struggle with visibility due to tools not tailored for comics, while readers face difficulty discovering standout content on cluttered, non-personalized platforms.

OBJECTIVE: Build a unified space where creators effortlessly showcase and monetize their comics, and readers discover and engage with interactive content that resonates.

Research & Insight

RESEARCH METHODS:

  • User interviews (comic artists and readers)
  • Competitive audit of existing platforms like Wattpad and GlobalComix

INSIGHT HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Wattpad offers reach but lacks comic-specific publishing tools.
  • Creators need simplified workflows; readers want personalized, interactive experiences.
  • GlobalComix enables monetization but has a niche audience, limiting discovery.
