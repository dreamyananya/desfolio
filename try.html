Doodle Tales | Every scribble tells a story


View on Behance

ROLE: UI/UX Designer | UX Researcher

TOOLS: Figma, Ibis Paint X, Adobe Capture

DURATION: 3 Weeks

QUICK IMPACT: A whimsical storytelling platform transforming simple doodles into interactive narratives that engage users creatively.


Problem & Objective

PROBLEM: Casual doodlers lack a playful, intuitive way to bring their scribbles to life as digital stories that are easy for kids to comprehend.

OBJECTIVE: Create a delightful, easy-to-use interface that transforms user sketches into shareable, narrative-driven experiences.

Research & Insight

RESEARCH METHODS: Informal user interviews (with doodle enthusiasts) and parents.

INSIGHT HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Users love creating but need features that spark imagination.
  • Stories should be accessible in all formats possible.

Concept & Flow

CONCEPT VISUALS: A UI based on doodle theme itself, that attracts kids.

USER FLOW AT A GLANCE::

Design Highlights

Hand-drawn textures, doodle-style icons, vibrant accents for landing page or product introduction while keeping the doodling UI clean and simple for kids to grasp.

Final Screens

Impact & Reflections

IMPACT (POTENTIAL):

  • Encouraged creative self-expression in a playful interface.
  • Boosted user engagement by making storytelling accessible to kids in video, audio as well as text format.


REFLECTION:

  • While curating for kids as primary users, clarity and playful styling should be balanced to keep their attention.
